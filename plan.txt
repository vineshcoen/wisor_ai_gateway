# Project Case Study: Wisor AI Clone - Intelligent Freight Quotation Platform

## 1. Problem Statement

Freight forwarding companies receive dozens or hundreds of shipment quote requests (RFQs) every day through email.

A typical email might look like:

```text
Hi Team,

Please provide rates for shipping 2 x 40FT containers from Ahmedabad, India to Hamburg, Germany.

Cargo: Textile
Weight: 20 Tons
Ready Date: Next Week

Regards,
ABC Exports
```

Currently, an operations executive must:

1. Read the email manually.
2. Identify shipment details.
3. Verify information is complete.
4. Search internal rate cards.
5. Calculate charges.
6. Prepare quotation.
7. Send quote back to customer.

This process can take anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes per RFQ.

When a company receives hundreds of RFQs daily, the operational cost becomes significant.

---

## Why Wisor Exists

Wisor's goal is not to "chat with users."

Its goal is:

> Convert unstructured freight inquiry emails into accurate quotations automatically.

The real value comes from:

* Faster quote generation
* Reduced human effort
* Consistent pricing
* Lower operational costs
* Faster response time to customers
